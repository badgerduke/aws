    8. 8. AWS Community Day | Cloud Costs | 8 Instance Sizing One of the advantages of the public cloud is buying only what you need. There is no quality discount as instances within a family get larger. Instance cost scale linearly until a price premium for the largest instances. Use autoscaling to handle variable traffic loads instead of oversizing your instances.
